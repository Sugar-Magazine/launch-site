About Sugar

Sugar is a feminist magazine that empowers young women to take initiative in everything they do.

Using poetry, fiction, personal essays, interviews, comics, drawings, and photography, Sugar will inspire its readers to fight against society's standards about how we are told to look and feel about ourselves.

Submit your work

Are you a poet, writer, artist, comic, or photographer? Are you an intersectional feminist? Do you aim to empower young women in all that they do?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, please submit your work to us!

Email submissions@thesugarmag.com

We are currently looking for insightful, vulnerable, personal artwork exploring gender, race, class, ability, and/or what it means to resist a society that attempts to devalue certain bodies and experiences. Any poems, personal essays, or visual art is strongly encouraged, as are submissions from queer & gender non-conforming individuals, individuals with disabilities, and people of color.
